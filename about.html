Our Story

Crafting Timeless Elegance Since 1985

Founded in the heart of Gaborone, Luxury Jewelry began as a small family workshop dedicated to creating exquisite handmade pieces. What started as a passion project between master jewelers has grown into Botswana's premier destination for fine jewelry.

For over three decades, we've combined traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design to create pieces that tell stories and become family heirlooms. Each creation reflects our commitment to quality, beauty, and ethical sourcing.

Today, while we've grown from that humble workshop, we maintain the same attention to detail and personal touch that defined our beginnings. Our team of skilled artisans continues the legacy of handcrafting jewelry that celebrates life's most precious moments.
